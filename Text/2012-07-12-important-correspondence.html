Important Correspondence

An important letter arrives from Antipodean Hooting Yard researcher Glyn Webster. The heading is “Supermarket”. I reproduce it in its entirety:

The elderly man ahead of me was buying a big bag of zeolite, two bottles of Shiraz and a funnel. And I said nothing! Lord knows what he has gone and done.
