Chrononhotondabblerus

‘Tis Friday morning, which means of course that one finds something new in my cupboard at The Dabbler. This week, a brief note on a forgotten drama by the author of God Save The King/Queen, Henry Carey (c.1687–1743), together with a link to the complete text. I hope this will be of interest to any clapped-out old thespians among my readership. Word has it that the Bodger's Spinney Variety Theatre will be mounting a star-studded production of the play in lieu of this year's Christmas panto.
