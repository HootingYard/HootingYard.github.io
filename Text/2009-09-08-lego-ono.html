Lego Ono

The Daily Beast has a set of “Classic Photography In Lego” by Mike Stimpson, a man with the good sense to include a snap of Yoko Ono. Her late husband, who I understand was a whiny Liverpudlian pop singer, is also in the shot, but sometimes one must take the chaff with the wheat.
