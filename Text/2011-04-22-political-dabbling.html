Political Dabbling

In The Dabbler this week I examine in breathtaking detail the parliamentary career of a scientist who dabbled in politics. Dabbler he may have been, but Sir Isaac Newton's record as a parliamentarian, albeit brief, was exemplary, and ought to serve as a model for some of the more loathsome specimens at large in the Palace of Westminster today.
