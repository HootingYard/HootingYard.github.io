The Twelve Days : Day Eleven

On the eleventh day of Christmas Old Halob gave to me

Eleven cornflake cartons,

Ten rusty nozzles,

Nine paper sickbags,

Eight tufts of bindweed,

Seven mordant herons,

Six hideous bat gods,

Ecto-oplasm!

Four dead chaffinches,

Three poptarts,

Two cardboard pigs,

And a viper, a shrew and a bee.
