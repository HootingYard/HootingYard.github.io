Coleridge, Roget, And Gas

Reviewing a new biography of Peter Mark Roget by Nick Rennison, Steven Poole notes “Roget studied medicine at Edinburgh University and began work at the wonderfully named Pneumatic Institution for Inhalation Gas Therapy, where he probably knew Coleridge, who loved to suck on the nitrous oxide”.
